    Meet Our committees

    HR Committee

    Responsible for recruiting, managing members, and ensuring effective teamwork inside the branch..

    FR Committee

    Responsible for building partnerships and securing sponsorships to support the branch financially.

    PR Committee

    Builds strong connections with external organizations, universities, and spreads awareness about the branch.

    Logistics Committee

    Manages event organization, resources, and ensures everything runs smoothly during activities and sessions..

    Programming Committee

    Focuses on software development, coding sessions, and technical projects that enhance members’ skills.

    Media Committee

    Creates and manages visual content, designs, photography, and social media presence for the branch.

    Core Committee

    The backbone of the branch, handling decision-making, supervision, and ensuring smooth operation of all activities..

    IOT Committee

    Specialized in Internet of Things projects, workshops, and innovation in connected devices and smart systems.

    F.A.Q

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How can I join the IEEE student branch?

    You can join by filling out the membership form on our website or attending one of our orientation sessions..

    Is there a fee for membership?

    Yes, membership requires a fee. This fee helps support the branch’s events and resources.

    What kind of events does the branch organize?

    The branch organizes workshops, competitions, technical seminars, and field visits regularly.

    How can I volunteer in branch activities?

    You can volunteer by logging into our website and signing up on the volunteers list or by contacting us directly via emai.
